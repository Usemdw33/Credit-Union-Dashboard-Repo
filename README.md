## Okta Authentication

You must be logged into Okta to use the front end. To initiate Okta login at Upstart, open a browser and go to the Upstart resource, such as upstart.okta.com, then enter your Upstart username and click Next. 

If you already have Okta Verify / FastPass set up, choose Use Okta FastPass and complete biometric verification; otherwise continue with your Upstart username and password.
